# Product_Now_Studio
A project at Industrial Engineering at the TU Delft: Students delve into sustainable product design strategies and methods, crafting environmentally responsible products.

3 students groups from the Master IPD worked on the case study Fizzy. The final reports, presentations and CAD files can be found here: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1GS3Di_8xom_RzG5fI1sxc40ySlFwCrmm?usp=sharing
